Well written book gives an in depth look into Indian family culture and values December 1, 2021

The Taste of Ginger is a first person narrative about an intelligent woman who was born into an upper class Indian culture but raised in America. The characterizations as well as the descriptions of the Indian culture are absolutely engrossing. Preeti Desai has spent her life from age seven trying assimilate into American culture. It is not until she is into her thirties and living the life of a typical American woman striving to get ahead in an old boys network that fate intervenes. A trip to Indian to deal with a family emergency results in an extended stay that enables her to finally reach the point of self-actualization. Her understanding of the conflicting values of her two cultures and her role in establishing her own priorities results in some life changing decisions. This book is a very enjoyable read. There is nothing in the way of graphic violence, sex, language or political leanings to offend any reader. I definitely would read another book by this author.
